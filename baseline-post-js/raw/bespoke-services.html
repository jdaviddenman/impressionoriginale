BESPOKE SERVICES

 I. CONSULTING & TRAINING

GIFT CEREMONY

Gift wrapping becomes a refined ceremony. Through in-depth research of your archives and technical drawings, we translate the heritage and values of your house with precision. By your side, we express your identity through a refined balance of modernity and authenticity.

Tailor-made development of your signature gift ceremony.

VISUAL IDENTITY

Visual identity is the visible expression of a brand’s essence. Aligned with heritage and authenticity, it must clearly reflect the values of the house. Impression Originale supports you from concept to final development, drawing on our know-how and the expertise of our creative collective.

Initial strategy or redesign : naming, brand identity, logo, product and packaging design.

Event strategy : concept and execution.

TRAINING

Transmitting excellence through gesture and technique. Impression Originale offers a validated educational approach focused on autonomy and precision. Training is conducted in small groups to ensure personalized, high-quality learning and transmission.

Training of trainers, small group sessions, visual and video supports for continuous learning.

 II. EVENT

ANIMATION

Celebrating the art of gifting through live experiences. Our talents accompany your events with carefully curated activities, designed according to your needs and desired duration. We create immersive moments that highlight your universe and savoir-faire.

Gift wrapping, hot embossing (leather, paper, fabric), calligraphy, engraving, sculpted paper (3D), drawing, feathers, embroidery, fabric weaving…

SET DESIGN

A set to be experienced, alive under our touch. We design unique artistic installations that transform spaces into immersive and engaging sensory experiences. Through an exploration of materials and forms, emotion and imagination naturally emerge.

Ephemeral artistic installation or art installation open to the public.

SIGNATURE EXPERIENCE

Signature Experience is the art of blending precision and emotion. From concept to execution, we craft personalized, high-end experiences that reflect your brand’s essence. Every detail is carefully tailored to ensure an unforgettable, immersive journey.

Tailor-made development of your Signature Experience.

 III. GIFT CONCIERGERIE

GIFT WRAPPING

Transforming an object into a meaningful gift. Through our gift wrapping know-how, each detail enhances anticipation and emotion. Drawing on ancestral techniques from around the world, we create unforgettable gifting experiences.

On-demand gift wrapping (le Bon Marché workshop)


Tailor-made packaging by appointment. On-site service upon request.

BESPOKE

Personalization defines care. By drawing on exceptional craftsmanship, we elevate each gift into a lasting emotional memory. Materials, techniques and finishes are explored to create truly bespoke pieces.

Calligraphy, gold hot stamping, paper sculpture, engraving, haute couture embroidery, feather work, bespoke ribbons or cords, illustration and drawing…

CONCIERGERIE

A seamless, end-to-end gifting service. From sourcing the gift to its exceptional packaging and final delivery, we take care of every detail with precision and discretion. Flawless execution and impeccable quality of service.

Service available in Paris only. Delivery possible. Quote upon request only.
